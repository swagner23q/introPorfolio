Learning Bootstrap

This week at Epicodus, our lessons taught us the basics and then we began to learn how to utilize Boostrap. At first, it was quite intimidating as we went through the beginning steps while following along to video tutorials from Treehouse. After the videos, my partner and I actually ended up going through the videos step by step. It was incredibly satisfying to complete each step at our own pace and watch the changes we made. Soon after, once we finished setting up the website with the basic layout, we began to customize. Customizing is always the best part of creating a website, the possibilities are infinite!
